The Daily Bread

I created this logo and brand idea for two class assignments. I was able to combine a logo assignment with a flyer assignment to make a better portfolio piece. I was thinking that the bakery would make their products by hand and the whole store would be a warm community space. I went with a neutral brown color palette because it gives a calm open feeling to the customer that a bakery should have.
